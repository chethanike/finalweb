About Our Research Platform

Our platform represents a cutting-edge approach to hardware security evaluation, specifically designed for identifying vulnerabilities in embedded systems through advanced side-channel analysis techniques.

The system provides comprehensive support for power consumption analysis, execution timing measurements, and electromagnetic emission monitoring, enabling researchers to observe subtle information leakages during cryptographic operations.

With integrated machine learning capabilities, real-time data monitoring, and intuitive control interfaces, this platform serves as an essential tool for advancing hardware-level cybersecurity research and education.
