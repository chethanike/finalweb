Future Development

Pushing the boundaries of embedded security research and defense, our roadmap focuses on advancing both attack-side hardware capabilities and defensive masking techniques for a more secure digital world.

Our Vision for the Future

As we continue to push the boundaries of embedded security research and defense, our product roadmap focuses on advancing both the attack-side hardware capabilities and the defensive masking techniques.

Our comprehensive approach ensures that security researchers, educators, and developers have access to cutting-edge tools for both understanding vulnerabilities and implementing robust defenses.

Key Development Areas

Advanced Hardware Capabilities

Priority: High

Upgrading our portable side-channel attack device to support more sophisticated and real-time analysis across EM, power consumption, and timing side channels.

Real-Time EM Trace Capture

Integrating high-resolution oscilloscopes with Raspberry Pi platforms for dynamic electromagnetic emission capture.

Higher Resolution Sampling

Capturing power and timing data at finer granularity for deeper leakage analysis and vulnerability detection.

Cross-Algorithm Support

Extending support beyond DES to AES, RSA, and lightweight ciphers used in IoT devices.

Portable Interface & Logging

Adding onboard UI and logging for stand-alone operation and live trace visualization.

Smarter Machine Learning Models

Priority: High

Continuously improving our ML models that predict cryptographic keys from side-channel data with enhanced accuracy and efficiency.

Hybrid Deep Learning Architectures

Combining LSTM temporal modeling with CNN spatial pattern recognition for superior trace interpretation.

Hyperparameter Optimization

Fine-tuning batch sizes, learning rates, and epochs for efficient training and maximum accuracy.

Generalized Learning

Training models on broader datasets with diverse plaintexts and keys for robust cross-scenario performance.

Next-Gen Masking Technique

Priority: Medium

Enhancing our masking system to defend against advanced attacks including power and EM analysis beyond current time-based protection.

Universal Masking Compatibility

Developing algorithms to support multiple encryption schemes like AES and RSA for broader adoption.

Enhanced User Interface

Creating intuitive GUI for easy integration and testing by researchers and developers.

Dynamic Masking Strategies

Introducing random delays, fake operations, and format-preserving transformations for enhanced obfuscation.

Real-Time Protection Module

Deploying masking as a lightweight real-time protection layer in embedded systems.

End-to-End Security Framework

Priority: Medium

Creating a comprehensive and secure cryptographic ecosystem that integrates multiple layers of protection and verification.

Secure Key Management

Automated and tamper-resistant key generation, storage, and renewal mechanisms for enhanced security.

Integrity & Verification Layers

Adding hash-based integrity checks and secure boot mechanisms to detect tampering attempts.

Migration to Stronger Algorithms

Transitioning from DES to AES or other robust encryption standards for improved security.

Constant-Time Execution

Ensuring cryptographic functions execute in constant time to prevent timing-based attacks.

Community & Research Support

Priority: Low

Building a strong community around our research platform and providing comprehensive support for educational and research activities.

Open-Source Contributions

Making portions of our toolset and datasets available for academic use and community development.

Educational Resources

Offering comprehensive documentation, tutorials, and workshops for researchers, educators, and students.

Modular Development Platform

Providing modular components for easy integration into custom hardware and research setups.
